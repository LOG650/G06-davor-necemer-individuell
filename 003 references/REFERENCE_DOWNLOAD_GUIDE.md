## Download Links & Access Methods

### **TIER 1: Free/Open Access (Download Immediately)**

#### 1. Hyndman & Athanasopoulos (2021) – Forecasting: Principles and Practice
- **Type:** Online textbook (free)
- **URL:** https://otexts.com/fpp3/
- **How to save:** 
  - Option A: Browse to chapters 9, 9.9, 10 and screenshot/save as PDF
  - Option B: Book is available as HTML; use browser's "Save as HTML" feature
  - Option C: Recommended—read online and cite with chapter numbers
- **Folder:** `01_Forecasting_Theory/`
- **Citation:** (Hyndman & Athanasopoulos, 2021, Ch. 9)

#### 2. Hyndman & Khandakar (2008) – Automatic ARIMA Forecasting
- **Title:** "Automatic time series forecasting: The forecast package for R"
- **Journal:** Journal of Statistical Software, 27(3)
- **DOI:** https://doi.org/10.18637/jss.v027.i03
- **How to access:** 
  - Direct PDF: https://www.jstatsoft.org/article/view/v027i03/v027i03.pdf
- **Folder:** `01_Forecasting_Theory/`
- **Citation:** (Hyndman & Khandakar, 2008)

#### 3. Arunraj, Ahrens & Fernandes (2016) – SARIMAX for Food Retail
- **Title:** "Application of SARIMAX Model to Forecast Daily Sales in Food Retail Industry"
- **Journal:** International Journal of Operations Research and Information Systems, 7(2)
- **How to access:**
  - ResearchGate: https://www.researchgate.net/publication/296477650_Application_of_SARIMAX_Model_to_Forecast_Daily_Sales_in_Food_Retail_Industry
  - Check if your university has IGI Global access
- **Folder:** `02_Food_Retail_Forecasting/`
- **Citation:** (Arunraj, Ahrens & Fernandes, 2016)

#### 4. Fildes, Ma & Kolassa (2022) – Retail Forecasting Review
- **Title:** "Retail forecasting: Research and practice"
- **Journal:** International Journal of Forecasting, 38(4), 1283–1318
- **DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1016/j.ijforecast.2022.04.004
- **How to access:**
  - ScienceDirect: https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0169207022000851
  - ResearchGate (often free version): https://www.researchgate.net/publication/360680289_Retail_forecasting_Research_and_practice

- **Citation:** (Fildes, Ma & Kolassa, 2022)

## How to Organize Locally

1. **Create folder structure** as shown above
2. **Save PDFs** with consistent naming: `LASTNAME_FIRSTNAME_YEAR_SHORTTITLE.pdf`
   - Example: `Hyndman_Rob_2021_Forecasting_Principles_Practice_Ch9.pdf`
3. **Create README.txt** in each subfolder listing:
   - Which sources are in this folder
   - What each is used for
   - Full citation for each
4. **Keep online resources as links** in a `LINKS.txt` file (don't try to "download" HTML unless you can save as PDF)

## Access Troubleshooting

| Source | Problem | Solution |
|--------|---------|----------|
| "Paywall – need subscription" | Journal behind paywall | Try ResearchGate, contact authors, check HiM library access |
| "Can't find on ScienceDirect" | Limited preview | Try DOI link directly, or use Google Scholar to find free version |
| "ResearchGate version outdated" | Preprint ≠ published version | Verify page numbers and publication details match official journal |
| "Winston textbook restricted" | Textbook expensive | Use generic LP concepts from Winston; cite (Winston, 2004) without exact page |
| "NNN document not public" | Labor agreement private | Contact NNN or your organization's HR; cite as (NNN, 2024–2026) based on data sheet |
